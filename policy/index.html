Privacy Policy

Last updated — February 2025

What we collect

When you register for a bidfolk event through LinkedIn, we collect your name and email address.

How we use your information

We use your information to send you invitations to bidfolk community events, which happen approximately twice a month. That's it.

We will never use your information for newsletters, marketing, or any other purpose without asking your permission first.
Third-party sharing

We do not share, sell, or provide your personal information to third parties. Your data stays with bidfolk.

How your data is stored

Your information is stored securely and is only accessible to the bidfolk team.

Your rights

You can request to see, update, or delete your information at any time. Just email us at heather@bidfolk.com and we'll take care of it.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, reach out to us at heather@bidfolk.com.
